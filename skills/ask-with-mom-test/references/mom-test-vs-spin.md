# Mom Test vs SPIN In 有膜有漾 Sales

The two skills should be used together, but they do different jobs.

## Division Of Labor

| Skill | Job | Best Moment |
|---|---|---|
| `ask-with-mom-test` | Clean the question so it is not leading, vague, or praise-seeking. | Before discovery, when the customer says "随便了解一下" or the sales question sounds biased. |
| `diagnose-with-spin-selling` | Develop the customer's need from context to problem to consequence to desired payoff. | After a real past behavior, pain, or goal has surfaced. |
| `recommend-film-product` | Match the need to 有膜有漾 products and packages. | After need, vehicle, and budget direction are clear. |

## Recommended Flow

1. Mom Test: ask about past behavior and concrete facts.
2. SPIN: deepen the problem and desired payoff.
3. Product recommendation: map to car film category and package.
4. Commitment: ask for the smallest useful next step.

## Example

Customer says:

```text
我就是随便了解一下车衣。
```

Mom Test response:

```text
您之前有没有遇到过车漆被剐蹭、补漆色差，或者高速石子打到前杠机盖的情况？
```

If customer says:

```text
之前旧车补过漆，颜色看着不一样。
```

SPIN follow-up:

```text
那这台新车您更想避免补漆色差，还是主要担心日常停车剐蹭？如果能把原车漆状态保护好，对您后续使用和保值是不是更重要？
```

Recommendation handoff:

```text
核心诉求是保护原车漆和减少补漆色差风险，可以进入 `recommend-film-product` 推荐隐形车衣方案。
```

## Anti-patterns

- Using SPIN before the question quality is clean.
- Asking "Would you buy" before any concrete pain appears.
- Treating compliments as readiness.
- Jumping to product specs after one vague problem statement.
- Forgetting to ask for advancement after a good conversation.
