# Good / Bad Questions For Car Film Sales

Use this table to rewrite leading or low-quality sales questions into questions that produce concrete customer data.

| Bad Question | Why It Fails | Better Question |
|---|---|---|
| 您觉得我们这个车衣好不好？ | Asks for opinion and invites politeness. | 您之前有没有遇到过车漆剐蹭、补漆色差或太阳纹？当时怎么处理的？ |
| 如果价格合适，您会贴吗？ | Future hypothetical; almost everyone can say yes. | 您之前了解过哪些贴膜方案？最后没贴的主要原因是什么？ |
| 您是不是也担心车漆被刮？ | Leading question; reveals the answer sales wants. | 平时最让您担心的漆面问题是什么？高速石子、停车剐蹭还是鸟粪树胶？ |
| 夏天车里是不是很热？ | Leading and too easy to agree with. | 去年夏天开这台车时，您觉得最明显的问题是晒、热、眩光，还是后排隐私？ |
| 我们这个套餐很划算吧？ | Seeks praise, not decision data. | 您现在对比方案时，最看重隔热、质保、价格还是施工保障？ |
| 您会推荐朋友来贴吗？ | Weak future promise. | 身边有谁最近也在看贴膜？如果您觉得合适，方便之后把案例转给他看看吗？ |
| 您喜欢这个颜色吗？ | Opinion without buying context. | 您之前有没有改过车色？这次更想低调耐看，还是明显换风格？ |
| 您觉得要不要贴车衣？ | Pushes the decision back to the customer without context. | 这台车后续保值、原漆状态和日常养护，您最在意哪一个？ |

## Rewrite Formula

1. Remove your product or preferred answer.
2. Ask about the customer's past behavior.
3. Ask for a specific example.
4. Ask what they tried and what was inconvenient.
5. Ask for a concrete next step only after a real need appears.

## Weak Signal Detection

- Compliment: “你们挺专业的。” -> Thank and ask about their actual situation.
- Fluff: “以后肯定会考虑。” -> Ask what would need to happen next.
- Generic need: “想隔热好一点。” -> Ask when heat bothered them most and who was in the car.
- Concrete need: “孩子坐后排晒，去年夏天很明显。” -> Strong enough to move into SPIN or recommendation.
- Commitment: “我周六到店看样膜。” -> Strong progress; prepare recommendation and next step.
