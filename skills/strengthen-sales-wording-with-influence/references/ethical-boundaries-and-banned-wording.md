# Ethical Boundaries And Banned Wording

This skill must improve trust, not pressure.

## Banned Without Verification

- "今天不定就没了"
- "只剩最后一个名额"
- "很多客户都选这个"
- "这是全网最低价"
- "百分百不会出问题"
- "贴了绝对不会被刮"
- "我们比所有品牌都好"
- "不用考虑，直接定"
- "别人都贴了，你不贴就亏了"
- "这个活动我只给你一个人"

## Required Rewrites

| Unsafe Intent | Safer Direction |
|---|---|
| Create urgency | Confirm real store schedule or suggest viewing samples. |
| Invent popularity | Use only real case material supplied by the store. |
| Push highest tier | Present enough option and upgrade option. |
| Attack competitor | Compare verifiable dimensions. |
| Overpromise protection | Explain what the film helps reduce or protect against. |
| Hide risk | Explain risk controls and aftercare boundaries. |

## Safe Boundary Language

- "具体价格和活动以门店当天政策为准。"
- "库存和排期需要门店确认。"
- "质保按具体产品和来源页确认。"
- "是否适合升级，要看车型、预算和用车环境。"
- "这不是让您马上定，而是先帮您判断哪一档更合适。"

## Red Flags In Draft Copy

- Absolute words: 绝对、百分百、永远、一定.
- Fake consensus: 大家都、很多人都、所有客户.
- Pressure words: 赶紧、马上定、不然亏.
- Unverified claims: 最低价、独家、官方认证、最后名额.

When these appear, remove them or replace them with a verifiable fact and a low-pressure next step.
