# Assignment Selling For Car Film

Assignment Selling means giving useful education before the next sales step so the customer arrives informed and the conversation becomes more efficient.

## When To Use

- Before a store visit.
- Before a quote call.
- Before comparing two packages.
- After a customer asks a hard FAQ.
- Before asking for a concrete next step.

## Car Film Assignments

| Moment | Send / Ask | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| Customer asks "多少钱" | Send price-factor explanation and ask for model + budget | Prevent blind quote shopping |
| Customer asks "有没有必要贴车衣" | Send car paint risk FAQ and ask about parking/highway/new-car status | Clarify real protection need |
| Customer asks "会不会伤车" | Send computer-cutting and construction-risk explanation | Build trust before store visit |
| Customer compares packages | Send two-option comparison: enough vs upgrade | Help customer self-select |
| Customer hesitates after praise | Ask for sample viewing, model confirmation, or store visit | Convert praise into advancement |
| Customer books construction | Send aftercare reminders | Reduce preventable aftercare issues |

## Suggested Message Pattern

```markdown
我先发您一段透明说明，方便您判断是不是适合您这台车。您看完后只需要回我两个信息：车型/年份，以及您更在意 {保护/隔热/隐私/外观/预算} 哪一个。我再帮您做一版更准确的方案。
```

## What Counts As Advancement

- Customer sends vehicle model/year.
- Customer picks a budget range.
- Customer chooses between two package directions.
- Customer agrees to view sample film.
- Customer books a store visit.
- Customer asks for a formal quote.
- Customer shares another decision-maker or introduces a friend.

## What Does Not Count

- "挺好的"
- "我考虑一下"
- "以后有需要找你"
- "如果便宜点就贴"
- "你们看起来很专业"

Treat these as weak signals and ask for a small concrete next step.
