# Tactical Empathy For Car Film Sales

Use tactical empathy to make the customer feel understood before asking for a decision. It is especially useful when the customer feels sold to, judged for budget, or uncertain about whether film is worth it.

## Core Moves

| Move | Car Film Use | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Mirror | Invite the customer to explain a loaded phrase. | 客户：“别家便宜很多。” 销售：“便宜很多？” |
| Label | Name the concern calmly. | “听起来您不是单纯看价格，是担心花了钱效果不明显。” |
| Accusation audit | Say the distrust out loud first. | “您可能会担心我接下来只是想推荐更贵的套餐。” |
| Calibrated question | Let the customer define a workable path. | “怎样的对比方式，会让您更容易判断哪一档值得？” |
| Summary | Confirm facts plus emotion. | “您主要是新车想保护原漆，但预算不想超太多，也担心售后麻烦，对吗？” |

## Suggested Flow

1. Mirror or label the customer's phrase.
2. Pause and let the customer add detail.
3. Ask one calibrated question.
4. Summarize the customer's real concern.
5. Offer a small next step.

## Car Film Examples

### Price Resistance

```markdown
“听起来您主要担心的是，花这个预算到底值不值。这样，我们先不急着定套餐，我把基础保护和升级保护按材料、施工、质保和适合人群给您对比一下，您看哪一档更贴近您的用车情况。”
```

### Hesitation

```markdown
“听起来您不是完全不考虑，而是还差一点确定感。您现在最想确认的是价格、效果、质保，还是施工会不会影响车？”
```

### Family Decision

```markdown
“理解，这类消费通常家里也会一起看。您家人更关心预算、保护效果，还是后期售后？我可以按这个重点帮您整理一版更容易对比的内容。”
```

### Competitor Comparison

```markdown
“别家便宜很多？那我们先把对比维度拆开看：材料、厚度、施工方式、质保、售后和是否适合您的车。您现在最想先对比哪一项？”
```

## Common Mistakes

- Explaining too early before the customer feels heard.
- Asking too many questions at once.
- Treating hesitation as rejection.
- Defending price before knowing what the customer is comparing.
- Summarizing only facts and missing the emotional concern.
